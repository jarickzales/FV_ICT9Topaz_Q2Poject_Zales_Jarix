Chicken Adobo

Chicken Adobo is one of the most popular dishes in the Philippines and might even be considered as the national dish of the Philippines. This dish is prepared by stewing chicken in vinegar and soy sauce along with bay leaves and pepper corns.

RECIPE

Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 2 lbs.
Dried Bay leaves 3 Pieces
Soy sauce 4 Tbsp.
White vinegar 6 Tbsp.
Garlic 5 Cloves
Water 1 ½ cup
Cooking Oil 3 Tbsp.
Sugar 2 tsp.
Salt ¼ tsp.
Whole Peppercorn 1 tsp.

Instructions

  1. 1. Heat the cooking oil in a pot and saute the garlic, onion, and ginger until the onion softens.
  2. 2. Add chicken. Continue sauteing for 2 minutes or until turns brown.
  3. 3. Pour 2 tablespoons of fish sauce and stir.
  4. 4. Add rice wash into cooking pot. Let it boil then simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. 5. Add water as needed. Boil.
  6. 6. Put green papaya into the pot. Keep simmering for 15-20 minutes.
  7. 7. Add Maggi Magic Chicken Cubes and stir. Turn off the heat then add the malunggay leaves. Cover for two minutes.
  8. 8. Transfer to serving bowl and enjoy!!!
