BREAKFAST


Hotsilog

Hotsilog is a meal composed of hotdogs, garlic fried rice, and fried egg. In a Filipino household, this is commonly eaten for breakfast with a condiment of banana ketchup and some pickled shredded papaya (atchara) on the side.

RECIPE

Ingredients Quantity
Tender Juicy Hotdogs 3 Pieces
Eggs 2 Pieces
Garlic Fried Rice 1 Cup
Tomato 1 pc, Medium, Sliced
Water 3/4 Cup
Cooking Oil 6 Tbsp.

Instructions

  1. 1. Pour water in a small pot and let it boil.
  2. 2. Add in the hotdogs and add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil. Cook until water evaporates.
  3. 3. Once the water has evaporated, let the hotdogs fry in the oil for 1 to 2 minutes while slowly stirring.
  4.     Once done, set aside
  5. 4. Prepare the eggs as you heat 4 tablespoons of cooking oil.
  6. 5. Once oil is hot, fry the egg.
  7. 6. Plate your hotdogs, eggs, and garlic rice and put some slices of tomatoes on the side.
  8. 7. Enjoy!!!!
