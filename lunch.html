Chicken Tinola

Chicken Tinola is a Filipino dish that is traditionally cooked with wedges of papaya and maulnggay leaves. The secret in making good tingle is to simmer the chicken for a long period of time. This extracts the flavor from the chicken and also makes the chicken tender.

RECIPE

Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 3 lbs. cut into serving sizes
Green Papaya 2 Pieces, sliced into wedges
Malunggay 1 Â½ cup
Garlic 5 cloves
Onion 1 Whole
Ginger 3 Thumbs, Jullienne cut
Maggi Magic Chicken Cubes 2 Pieces
Fish sauce (patis) 2 Tbsp.
Cooking oil 3 Tbsp.
Rice wash 1 quart

Instructions

  1. 1. Combine chicken, soy sauce, and garlic in a bowl and mix well. Marinate for at least one hour.
  2. 2. Heat a cooking pot and pour the cooking oil.
  3. 3. Once heated, pan-fry the marinated chicken for 2 minutes on each side.
  4. 4. Pour the remaining marinade into the pot, add water, and boil.
  5. 5. Add dried bay leaves and whole peppercorn and let it simmer for 30 minutes or until chicken is tender.
  6. 6. Add vinegar then stir and cook for 10 minutes.
  7. 7. Put the sugar and salt in, stir, and then turn off the heat.
  8. 8. Enjoy!!!
